What We Do

Empowering Women:

Tha'avun is committed to empowering educated unemployed women by offering a unique blend of opportunities. Eliminating entry and registration fees, our employment generation initiative enables women to evolve from promoters to instructors, fostering financial independence through traditional and vocational skills.

Employment Generation:

Our commitment to employment generation extends beyond traditional tutoring. Tha'avun provides classes in diverse skills such as ornament making, tailoring, parenting, language proficiency, motivational sessions, and more. This multifaceted approach not only addresses unemployment but also nurtures a community of skilled individuals contributing to various sectors.

High-Quality Classes:

Tha'avun is dedicated to delivering high-quality classes, spanning traditional subjects and diverse skill-building courses. In addition to conventional tuitions, our instructors undergo comprehensive training in areas like personality development, calligraphy (Arabic), and public examination special tuitions. Students can expect a holistic learning experience that goes beyond textbooks, fostering a passion for continuous learning.

Community Impact:

Tha'avun's impact extends into the community through employment generation classes, creating avenues for skill development, financial independence, and personal growth. We believe in offering not just an education but a transformative experience that positively influences both our instructors and students.
